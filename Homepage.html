About Me

Hi, I'm Matt and I am studying a BA(Hons) in Media and Communication at Birmingham City University.

I was born and raised in Worcester where I attended Lyppard Grange Primary School, Blessed Edward Oldcorne Catholic College and Worcester Sixth Form College. I only really found my passion for media and technology production in college, but having had a background in performing arts, being parts of shows and competing in competitions, this helps with creativity, team work and productions. But I have also always had a keen interest in technology, therefore a combination of the 2 was media and that is how I came to where I am today.

At university I am currently completing my first year. During this time I have completed 4 production modules: TV Location, TV Studio, Photography and New Media. Alongside these I also have theory modules and we are in the process of putting on a media festival to showcase our work at the end of the academic year. I am loving it!

The media industry is very exciting and ever changing, therefore this is the path I want to pursue. My ambitions for the future are to develop my skills further, create unique content and share my work and experiences with you!
